> **Electricity Maps Carbon Display**
> `data science` `python` `electricity`
>
> Raspberry Pi project to display the current share of renevable energy production and the carbon level in Germany. I used the [Electricitymaps API](https://app.electricitymaps.com/developer-hub/api/getting-started). The integration is super simple and the basic request are available for free. ty EM <3. Hooked up everything to a Raspberry Pi with a little display. The script is now running on a regular base using a cronjob to display the data in a 15 minute resolution.
